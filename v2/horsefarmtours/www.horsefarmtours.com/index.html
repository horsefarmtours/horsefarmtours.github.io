Come horse around with us!

Let us show you the Horse Capital like it was meant to be seen!

If you are looking for a close encounter with horses, this tour is a must! Horse Farm Tours gives its guests a close-up look at the world's premier Thoroughbred horse farms. You can expect to spend approximately three hours with your tour guide visiting horse farms, Historic Keeneland Race Course and other attractions in the area. Every tour includes a drive by Calumet, two farms with a walk through one of the region's modern barns, and a tour of Keeneland Race Course. Tours depart from the Embassy Suites at Exit 115 on I-75 twice daily.

We offer a complete and comfortable tour with knowledgeable guides. We strive to make your visit to the Horse Capital of the World a part of the great Thoroughbred tradition. Our goal is to take you inside the plank fences and down the shady lanes of Central Kentucky. You will see horses after their racing careers are over, in-foal broodmares, weanlings, yearlings and depending upon the time of year, newborn foals. Our tour is unique in that we offer a glimpse into the daily procedures of working farms. You will have an opportunity to walk through the modern barns. You don't even have to clean a stall!

About Us
Located in the Horse Capital of the World, Horse Farm Tours, Inc is owned and operated by a native Lexingtonian. We have been in business for 35 years. Our tour directors are well-versed in the horse industry and local attractions.  They are constantly reading and doing research on the horse industry.
