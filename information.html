Tour Information

Everything you need to know about our tours

Tour Details

Duration

Our standard tours last approximately 3 hours. Private tours can be customized to your preferences.

Departure Location

Tours depart from the Embassy Suites at Exit 115 on I-75 twice daily.

What to Expect

  • Visit to Keeneland Race Course
  • Tour of two working horse farms
  • Drive by Calumet Farm
  • Walk through modern barns
  • See horses at various stages of life

What to Bring

Essential Items

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Weather-appropriate clothing
  • Camera or smartphone
  • Water bottle
  • Sunscreen (in summer)

Tour Policies

Cancellation Policy

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. No-shows will be charged the full tour price.

Age Requirements

Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. All ages are welcome on our tours.

Accessibility

Please contact us in advance if you have any special accessibility requirements.
