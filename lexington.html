Visiting Lexington

Explore the Horse Capital of the World

About Lexington

Lexington, Kentucky, is known as the "Horse Capital of the World" for good reason. Home to some of the most prestigious thoroughbred farms and racing facilities, Lexington offers visitors a unique opportunity to experience the heart of the horse industry.

Local Attractions

Keeneland Race Course

One of the most beautiful race tracks in the world, offering live racing in April and October.

Kentucky Horse Park

A working horse farm and educational theme park dedicated to man's relationship with the horse.

The Red Mile

Historic harness racing track and entertainment venue.
